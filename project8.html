Calculation of surface roughness of electrodeposited copper using Atomic Force Microscopy

Atomic Force Microscopy

A sample of electrodeposited copper was taken and analyzed for its surface roughness using the Gwyddion software.


Line profiles were extracted at different lengths and Ra and RMS values were compared with the analytical calculations done on Microsoft Excel.



The difference in calculated values was very negligible and thus validated the process.
